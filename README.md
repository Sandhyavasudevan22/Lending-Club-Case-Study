## General Information
- Provide general information about your project here.
- What is the background of your project? 
In the Business world lending loans to ‘risky’ applicants is the largest source of financial loss (called credit loss). Credit loss is the amount of money lost by the lender when the borrower refuses to pay or runs away with the money owed. In other words, borrowers who default cause the largest amount of loss to the lenders. In this case, the customers labelled as 'charged-off' are the 'defaulters'. 
If one can identify these risky loan applicants, then such loans can be reduced thereby cutting down the amount of credit loss. Identification of such applicants using EDA is the aim of this case study.


- What is the business problem that your project is trying to solve?
To identify the risk associated in lending a loan at the time of application so that the company can go forward with the approval or rejection of a loan
- What is the dataset that is being used?
Dataset is a csv fail containing various information about the loan sanctioned. It contains demographical factors like State Code and Zip Code, Loan Specific factors like loan amount, Loan interest etc. and behavioural factors like Delinq amount


## Conclusions
-Most of the customers that are applying for loan tend get default
-Default rate is higher on the total loan applications
-Customers get loan maximum with grade B,C,A and D than E,F and G
-Customers with grade B is maximum and with grade G the minimum
-Most customers take loan for consolidation and  major purchase 
-The customers with lesser loan amount tend to default more
-The customers with more annual income tend to default more
-The customers with less interest rate tend to default more
-The customers with less installments tend to default more
-The customers with grade 'G' tend to default more.
-The customer with 60 months term period tend to default more than 36 months term period
-The customer with home ownership classified as other tend to default more
-The loan got for the purpose of small business has the maximum chances for getting default and the least is major purchase
-The loan with higher loan amount tend to default more
-The loan with high dti tend to default more
-The Loans taken in the month of December tend to default more
-The year 2008 has maximum default rates and the year 2009 has the least default rate
